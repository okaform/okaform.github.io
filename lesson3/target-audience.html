Target Audience

bestweathersite.org is a website that can be used by anyone who is looking to find out more information about the weather in their area. It can be used by anyone who has mobile phones, tablets, and pcs. The people who will mainly be going to this site are people live in extreme cold weathers like Idaho and Utah, who need to constantly check the weather to know what kind of activities they will need to do that day and what kind of outfit they will need to wear. This site can also be used by anyone who wants to go on a vacation to another country and want to get a feel of what the weather is like there.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Why is bestweathersite.org important to me?

It is important to you because the site will help you get accurate weather information.

Scenario 2: Is there a preffered viewport that I should use when visiting bestweathersite?

There isn't. Our website has been designed to suite all posible users views. So, if you like using your phone, then it would work well for you.

Scenario 3: What are some of the design principles of bestweathersite?

We've applied some very important principles such as hick's law, fitt's law, rule of thirds, repitition in our shapes and much more!

Scenario 4: What are the main tenets of bestweathersite?

We belive that everyone should get the best and more accurate information a weather website can offer. So, we strive for perfection and customer happiness.

Scenario 5: What if bestweathersite crashes? How will you help?

For bestweathersite to crash, it would mean that the internet crashes. We have dozens of server all constantly running and ready to go if one fails. We also have more than one satalite gathering information for our site.
