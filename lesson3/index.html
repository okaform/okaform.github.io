Overview

The Site Purpose

The main purpose of this website: bestweathersite.org is to provide users with accurate ad-free weather information in their local areas. This site will also provide weather information in other international areas that they want to view. This objective will be achieved through making searching for weather reports an easy tasks, providing live local atmospheric conditions per locations, and providing users with the ability to change their temperature from celcius to fahrenheit. We want our users to be able to visit our site without any stress of finding vital information. If they want to know if they should plan an outdoor trip when it will rain the next day they will be able to find such information on bestweathersite.org.
